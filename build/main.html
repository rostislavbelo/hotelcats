Frequently asked questions

  • What materials are the jewellery made of?

    Our jewellerry is made of solid gold, silver or leather. Also, some jewellery contains precious and semiprecious stones and crystals.

  • Which countries do you deliver to?

    Our offices operate all over the world, with the exception of Antarctica, Somalia and North Korea. Our office in Honduras will open soon. The order is available through the website.

  • What are the payment methods?

    CH store accepts cash and cards for payment at retail points of sale. Online purchases are paid by cards of all leading payment systems: MIR-card, VISA, MasterCard, American Express.

  • Can I return the jewellery if it didn’t fit me?

    Our store respects consumer rights - you can return jewelry that did not suit you within 10 days after purchase, you must keep the presentation and original packaging.
